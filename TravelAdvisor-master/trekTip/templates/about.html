TrekTip

We believe there is a need for a product that empowers people to provide feedback on venues and events within any given region.

Furthermore, we believe this information should be presented in a simple interface such that the information is relevant and immediately consumable.

TrekTip provides a platform that is focused on Places, Attractions, and User Experience. Information is expanded and maintained by communities, local experts, venue staff and event managers.

TrekTip motivates and inspires communities to travel, visit and share.
